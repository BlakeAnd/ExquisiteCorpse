Exquisite Corpse

aka

Foldy Foldy Draw Draw

a drawing game for friends

How it works!

  • Get a computer and one friend with a computer.
  • You will get half the drawing
  • Send the link for the other half to your friend.
  • Create your drawings without seeing the other half.
  • Send your half of the drawing in.
  • See what you made together!
